Common Conditions We Treat

To find relief from your painful symptoms and condition, the first step is to find the cause. At the International Spine & Sports Institute, we diagnose and treat a wide range of spine conditions and sports-related injuries.

Sciatica

Sciatica is described as the painful symptoms associated with compression or irritation of the sciatic nerve.

LEARN MORE
Spinal Stenosis

Spinal stenosis occurs when the space in the spinal canal where the spinal cord lives narrows, resulting in pressure on or pinching of nearby nerves.

LEARN MORE
Herniated Disc

A herniated disc occurs when the soft inner layer of a spinal disc protrudes through the tough outer layer.

LEARN MORE
Bulging Disc

A bulging disc occurs when the tough exterior portion a spinal disc is compressed and extends beyond the edge of the vertebrae, affecting a nerve.

LEARN MORE
Lower Back Pain

Typically developing from overuse or another medical condition, lower back pain is a common symptom for a range of spine conditions.

LEARN MORE
Degenerative Disc Disease

This common spine condition is the natural wear and tear of spinal discs due to aging, but can be painful and problematic.
